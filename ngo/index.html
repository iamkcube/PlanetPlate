About Us

ReSatiate is a noble initiative aimed at reducing food waste while simultaneously catering to the needs of the underprivileged. The page lists out the restaurants that have signed up to donate their leftover food and allows people to place requests for the same. The volunteers of the NGO collect the leftover food from the restaurants and distribute it among the needy people, ensuring that no good food goes to waste. By facilitating the sharing of surplus food, the NGO page serves as a platform that connects restaurants with people in need, ultimately promoting a culture of social responsibility and community welfare.
